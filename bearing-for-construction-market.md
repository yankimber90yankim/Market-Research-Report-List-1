<p><p>The construction market for bearings primarily includes ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings feature spherical balls that reduce friction between moving parts, offering smooth rotational motion. They are ideal for high-speed applications. Roller bearings utilize cylindrical rollers to handle heavy loads and provide greater surface area contact, making them suitable for high-load scenarios. Both types are essential in construction machinery, ensuring durability, efficiency, and reliability in equipment operation and longevity, contributing to overall productivity on construction sites.</p></p>
