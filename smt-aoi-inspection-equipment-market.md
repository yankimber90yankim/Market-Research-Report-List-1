<p><p>The SMT AOI (Surface Mount Technology Automated Optical Inspection) equipment market is divided into two primary types: inline and offline inspection systems. Inline systems are integrated directly into the production line, enabling real-time monitoring and defect detection without interrupting the manufacturing process. Each type offers distinct advantages, with inline providing immediate feedback and offline allowing for detailed analysis in a controlled environment.</p></p>
