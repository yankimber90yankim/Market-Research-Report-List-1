<p><p>The Swine Flu vaccine market comprises various administration methods: intradermal, intramuscular, subcutaneous, and others. Intradermal vaccination involves administering the vaccine into the skin, promoting a robust immune response with minimal dosage. Intramuscular vaccination injects the vaccine deep into muscle tissue for effective absorption and immunity. Subcutaneous vaccination delivers the vaccine into the fat layer beneath the skin, often resulting in a slower release. Other methods may include oral or nasal deliveries, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
