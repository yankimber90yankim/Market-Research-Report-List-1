<p><p>Metal Working Fluids (MWFs) are essential in industrial processes to enhance performance and tool life. **Metal Removal Fluids** are used in cutting, grinding, and drilling to reduce friction, cool tools, and remove debris. **Metal Treating Fluids** aid in heat treatment processes like quenching and tempering to alter metal properties. **Metal Forming Fluids** facilitate shaping operations like rolling and stamping by reducing wear and improving surface finish. **Metal Protecting Fluids** prevent corrosion and oxidation during storage or transportation, ensuring metal integrity. Each type serves specific functions in metalworking industries.</p></p>
