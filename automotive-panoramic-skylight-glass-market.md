<p><p>The automotive panoramic skylight glass market is segmented into two main types: external panoramic skylights and built-in panoramic skylights. External panoramic skylights are integrated into the vehicle's roof, enhancing aesthetics and providing an expansive view, often featuring sliding or tilting mechanisms for ventilation. Built-in panoramic skylights, on the other hand, are designed as a fixed component of the vehicle's structure, offering consistent light and a spacious feel without the option for opening. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and vehicle designs.</p></p>
