<p><ul><li>Constant Head Soil Permeability Apparatus</li><li>Falling Head Soil Permeability Apparatus</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Soil Permeability Apparatus market encompasses two primary types: Constant Head and Falling Head Soil Permeability Apparatus. The Constant Head apparatus is designed for measuring the permeability of coarse-grained soils by maintaining a constant water head during testing, suitable for high permeability materials. In contrast, the Falling Head apparatus is used for fine-grained soils, where the water level decreases over time, allowing for the assessment of lower permeability. Both methods are crucial for evaluating soil drainage characteristics and engineering applications.</p></p>
