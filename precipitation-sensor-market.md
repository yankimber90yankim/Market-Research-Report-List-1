<p><strong>The Precipitation Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tipping Bucket Precipitation Sensor</li><li>Weighing Precipitation Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precipitation sensor market primarily features two main types: tipping bucket precipitation sensors and weighing precipitation sensors. Tipping bucket sensors measure rainfall by collecting water in a bucket that tips once a predetermined amount is reached, allowing for accurate rainfall measurement over time. In contrast, weighing precipitation sensors utilize a scale to measure the weight of collected water, providing continuous data on precipitation levels. Both sensor types are essential for meteorological applications, agriculture, and hydrology, offering reliable rainfall monitoring solutions.</p></p>
