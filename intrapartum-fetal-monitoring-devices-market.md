<p><p>The intrapartum fetal monitoring devices market includes various technologies essential for assessing fetal well-being during labor. Intrauterine pressure catheters measure uterine contractions internally for precise strength assessments. Transducers for fetal heart rate (FHR) monitor the baby's heartbeat externally, while transducers for uterine contractions measure the frequency and intensity of contractions. Together, these devices ensure informed decision-making and enhance maternal and fetal safety during delivery.</p></p>
