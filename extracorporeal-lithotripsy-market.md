<p><strong>The Extracorporeal Lithotripsy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrohydraulic Technique</li><li>Electromagnetic Technique</li><li>Piezoelectric Eechnique</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extracorporeal lithotripsy market comprises three primary techniques: Electrohydraulic, Electromagnetic, and Piezoelectric. The Electrohydraulic technique utilizes high-voltage electrical discharges to generate shockwaves, effectively fragmenting stones. The Electromagnetic technique employs magnetic fields to create focused shockwaves, providing targeted treatment with minimal damage to surrounding tissues. Finally, the Piezoelectric technique uses piezoelectric crystals to produce ultrasound waves, ensuring precise stone disintegration. Each method offers distinct advantages, catering to varying patient needs and preferences in urological treatments.</p></p>
