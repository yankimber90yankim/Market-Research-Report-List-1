<p><p>The motorcycle protective helmet market includes various types designed for different riding needs. **Full Face Helmets** offer maximum protection, covering the entire head and face, ideal for high-speed and long-distance rides. **Off-Road Helmets** are lightweight with extended chin bars and visors, tailored for rugged terrains and motocross. **Open Face Helmets** cover the head but leave the face exposed, providing comfort and visibility for urban or casual riding. **Half Helmets** cover only the top of the head, offering minimal protection but maximum airflow and freedom.</p></p>
