<p><strong>The Vehicle Superchargers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Roots</li><li>Twin-Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Superchargers market includes three main types: Centrifugal, Roots, and Twin-Screw. Centrifugal superchargers use a compressor wheel to force air into the engine, offering high efficiency at higher RPMs. Roots superchargers employ twin rotors to push air directly into the intake, providing strong low-end torque. Twin-Screw superchargers use intermeshing screws to compress air, delivering a balance of efficiency and power across a wide RPM range. Each type caters to different performance needs, enhancing engine output and responsiveness.</p></p>
