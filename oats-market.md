<p><p>The oats market can be categorized into three main types: groats, regular flakes, and powder/flour. Groats are whole, hulled oats, retaining their nutritional value and fiber, often used in cooking and breakfast dishes. Regular flakes are processed oats that are steamed and flattened, making them suitable for quick cooking and popular in cereals. Powder or flour comprises finely ground oats, versatile for baking and thickening, catering to the demand for gluten-free and healthy alternatives in various recipes.</p></p>
