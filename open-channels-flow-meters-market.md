<p><strong>The Open Channels Flow Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weir Open Channels Flow Meters</li><li>Slot Open Channels Flow Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Open Channels Flow Meters are essential for measuring the flow of liquids in open channels, such as rivers, streams, and wastewater systems. Weir Open Channels Flow Meters use a weir, or barrier, to create a measurable drop in water level, enabling precise flow calculations. Slot Open Channels Flow Meters, on the other hand, utilize a narrow opening to measure flow based on the velocity of water through the slot. Both types are widely used in environmental monitoring and hydraulic engineering applications.</p></p>
