<p><p>The automotive pillars are structural components that support a vehicle's roof and enhance safety. The **A-Pillar** is located at the front, framing the windshield and housing airbags. The **B-Pillar** is between the front and rear doors, providing structural rigidity and side-impact protection. The **C-Pillar** is at the rear, supporting the rear window and roof. The **D-Pillar** is found in larger vehicles like SUVs or wagons, extending to the rear for additional stability. Each pillar plays a critical role in vehicle design, safety, and aerodynamics.</p></p>
