<p><p>An automotive sensor is a device that detects and measures physical properties such as temperature, pressure, speed, or position in vehicles. These sensors play a critical role in enhancing vehicle performance, safety, and efficiency by providing real-time data to electronic control units (ECUs) for optimal operation. Common types include temperature sensors, pressure sensors, position sensors, and motion sensors, which are integral to advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), engine management, and emission control.</p><p>The Automotive Sensor Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for connected and autonomous vehicles, stringent emission regulations, and the rising adoption of electric vehicles (EVs). Additionally, the shift toward electric and hybrid vehicles is creating new opportunities for sensors that monitor battery health and energy management. These factors are shaping the future of the automotive sensor market, making it a dynamic and rapidly evolving industry.</p></p>
