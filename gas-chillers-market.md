<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas chillers are categorized into two main types: open loop and closed loop systems. Open loop gas chillers use ambient air or external water sources for cooling, typically offering high efficiency in suitable climates. In contrast, closed loop gas chillers recirculate a refrigerant within a sealed system, providing greater control over cooling and minimizing environmental impact. Both types cater to different applications, with open loop systems often being more cost-effective while closed loop systems excel in efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
