<p><p>The perfume market consists of different fragrance concentrations catering to varying preferences and occasions. Eau de Cologne features a lower concentration, often refreshing and suitable for warmer climates. Eau Fraiche is the lightest, providing a subtle fragrance for a brief period.</p></p>
