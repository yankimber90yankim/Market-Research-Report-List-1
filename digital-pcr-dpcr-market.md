<p><ul><li>Microfluidic dPCR</li><li>Droplet Digital PCR (ddPCR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital PCR (dPCR) market includes two primary types: microfluidic dPCR and droplet digital PCR (ddPCR). Microfluidic dPCR utilizes small channels to manipulate fluid volumes, allowing precise partitioning of samples for accurate quantification. In contrast, ddPCR segregates samples into thousands of droplets, each serving as an individual reaction compartment, enhancing sensitivity and precision. Both technologies facilitate highly efficient nucleic acid quantification, finding applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and pathogen detection, driving innovation and growth in the molecular biology field.</p></p>
