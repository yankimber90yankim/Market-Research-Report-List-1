<p><p>Industrial cameras for the medical and life sciences market primarily consist of area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture entire images at once, making them suitable for applications requiring high-resolution images of static subjects, like tissue analysis and microscopy. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, which is ideal for inspecting moving samples or surfaces, such as in automated lab equipment. Both types enhance imaging capabilities for diagnostics and research.</p></p>
