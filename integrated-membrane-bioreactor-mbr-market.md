<p><p>The Integrated Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) market comprises three main membrane types: hollow fiber, flat sheet, and multi-tubular membranes. Hollow fiber membranes offer a high surface area in a compact design, ideal for space-limited applications. Flat sheet membranes provide easier cleaning and maintenance, often used in large-scale operations. Multi-tubular membranes combine durability and efficiency, suitable for various industrial processes. Each type caters to specific user needs, enhancing water treatment efficiency and overall system performance.</p></p>
