<p><p>The ink resin market comprises various types, each suited for different applications. Modified rosin resins provide excellent adhesion and gloss, commonly used in printing inks. Hydrocarbon resins enhance pigment dispersion and are favored for high-performance inks. Acrylic resins offer versatility and durability, suitable for both water-based and solvent-based inks. Polyamide resins improve heat resistance and flexibility, ideal for specialty inks. Polyurethane resins deliver superior abrasion resistance and chemical stability, making them suitable for diverse printing technologies.</p></p>
