<p><p>Hydrochloric acid electrolysis involves splitting HCl into hydrogen and chlorine using various technologies. **ODC Electrolysis** uses oxygen-depolarized cathodes, enhancing efficiency by reducing energy consumption. **Dupont Gas Phase Electrolysis** operates in a gas-phase environment, offering high purity and scalability. **Diaphragm Electrolysis** employs a porous diaphragm to separate products, ensuring minimal contamination. The **Sumitomo Process** integrates advanced membrane technology, optimizing energy use and product yield. Each method caters to specific industrial needs, balancing efficiency, cost, and environmental impact in chlorine and hydrogen production.</p></p>
