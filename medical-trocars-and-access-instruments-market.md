<p><p>Medical trocars and access instruments are essential tools used in minimally invasive surgeries to create entry points into the body. Trocars are sharp-pointed instruments that facilitate the insertion of other instruments or cameras into a patient's body, while access instruments are designed to assist in the establishment and maintenance of access during surgical procedures. These devices are crucial for laparoscopic surgeries, which have gained popularity due to benefits like reduced postoperative pain, shorter hospital stays, and quicker recovery times.</p><p>The Medical Trocars and Access Instruments Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period.
