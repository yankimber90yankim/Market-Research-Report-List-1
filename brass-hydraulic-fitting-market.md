<p><p>The brass hydraulic fitting market features various types of joints, including sleeve type and welded pipe joints. Sleeve type pipe joints offer ease of installation and maintenance, utilizing a sliding sleeve to connect pipes without the need for welding, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility. In contrast, welded pipe joints provide a strong, permanent connection by fusing the pipes together, ensuring durability and leak resistance. Both types cater to different needs in hydraulic systems, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
