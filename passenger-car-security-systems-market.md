<p><ul><li>Immobilizer</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry (RKE)</li><li>Passive Keyless Entry (PKE)</li><li>Passive Keyless Go (PKG)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger car security systems encompass various technologies designed to enhance vehicle safety. The immobilizer prevents the engine from starting without the correct key, thwarting theft attempts. Remote Keyless Entry (RKE) allows drivers to unlock doors or start the car from a distance using a key fob. Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) automatically unlocks the vehicle when the key fob is nearby, improving convenience. Passive Keyless Go (PKG) facilitates keyless starting, allowing drivers to start the engine without physically inserting the key.</p></p>
