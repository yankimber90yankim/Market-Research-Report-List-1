<p><p>Veneers are cosmetic dental enhancements used to improve the appearance of teeth. The market is primarily divided into two types: composite material and dental porcelain veneers. Composite veneers are made of resins that are molded to the tooth's shape, offering a cost-effective and quick solution but with less durability. In contrast, dental porcelain veneers are crafted from high-quality ceramic, providing superior aesthetic appeal and longevity, making them suitable for patients seeking a more permanent and natural-looking enhancement.</p></p>
