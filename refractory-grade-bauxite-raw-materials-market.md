<p><p>The refractory grade bauxite raw materials market is primarily categorized into two types based on the calcination process used: shaft furnace calcining bauxite and converter calcining bauxite. Shaft furnace calcining bauxite involves a continuous process in vertical shafts, producing high-purity bauxite suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, converter calcining bauxite utilizes a more dynamic process, often yielding bauxite with unique characteristics tailored for specific refractory applications, such as steelmaking and ceramics. Both types cater to distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
