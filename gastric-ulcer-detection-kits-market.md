<p><ul><li>Urea Breath Detection Kit</li><li>Fecal Detection Kit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gastric ulcer detection kits are essential tools for diagnosing peptic ulcers linked to Helicobacter pylori infection. Two prominent types are the urea breath detection kit and the fecal detection kit. The urea breath detection kit measures carbon dioxide in exhaled breath after the ingestion of urea, indicating H. pylori activity. In contrast, the fecal detection kit identifies antigens or genetic material from H. pylori in stool samples, providing a non-invasive, efficient method for diagnosis and monitoring of gastric ulcers.</p></p>
