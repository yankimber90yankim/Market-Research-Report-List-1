<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) Motor market includes four main types: </p><p>1. **Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS)**: Integrated into the steering column, offering compact design and enhanced responsiveness.</p><p>2. **Rear Electric Power Steering (REPS)**: Positioned at the rear axle, improving vehicle maneuverability and stability, particularly in larger vehicles.</p><p>3. **Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS)**: Directly connected to the rack and pinion, providing precise steering control and reduced weight.</p><p>4. **Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)**: Combines electric motors with hydraulic systems for improved efficiency and performance, bridging traditional hydraulic and fully electric systems.</p></p>
