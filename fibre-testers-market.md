<p><p>The fibre testers market encompasses various specialized equipment used to evaluate fabric properties. Tensile testers measure the strength and stretch of materials under tension. Abrasion testers assess durability against wear and friction. Color fastness testers evaluate how resistant dyes are to washing, light, and rubbing. Air permeability testers gauge the fabric's ability to allow air flow, while waterproofness testers determine resistance to water penetration.
