<p><ul><li>Flange Type</li><li>Welding Type</li><li>Groove Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pump connector market is categorized into three main types: flange type, welding type, and groove type. Flange type connectors provide a bolted connection, ensuring a secure and leak-proof joint, making them popular in high-pressure applications. Welding type connectors offer a permanent bond through welding, ideal for systems requiring durability. Groove type connectors use a groove and clamp mechanism for easy assembly and disassembly, providing flexibility for maintenance. Each type is tailored for specific application needs and installation conditions.</p></p>
