<p><p>Industrial PCs (IPCs) are ruggedized computing devices designed for industrial environments, offering reliability and performance. The market can be categorized into fanless and fanned industrial computers. Fanless IPCs utilize passive cooling, making them ideal for dusty or noisy settings, enhancing durability. In contrast, fanned IPCs feature active cooling systems, allowing for higher performance levels in demanding applications. Both types cater to various industries, including manufacturing, automation, and transportation, ensuring seamless operation under harsh conditions while meeting specific computing needs.</p></p>
