<p><strong>The Hand held Veterinary Refractometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Refractometers</li><li>Digital Refractometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld veterinary refractometers market is categorized into two main types: optical refractometers and digital refractometers. Optical refractometers use light refraction to measure the concentration of solutes in a liquid sample, providing a direct reading via an eyepiece. In contrast, digital refractometers utilize sensors and electronic displays to provide precise measurements quickly and easily, often featuring automatic temperature compensation. Both types are essential for assessing the hydration levels and overall health of animals in veterinary practice.</p></p>
