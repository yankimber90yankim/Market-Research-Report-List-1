<p><strong>The Aircraft Tugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Towbars</li><li>Towbarless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft tugs market comprises two primary types: conventional towbars and towbarless systems. Conventional towbars connect to the aircraft's nose gear, allowing for traditional towing practices. They are cost-effective and widely used. In contrast, towbarless tugs provide a more versatile solution, using a mechanical coupling to lift the aircraft without a physical towbar, enhancing maneuverability and reducing wear on landing gear. Each type serves distinct operational needs within ground handling and aircraft maintenance sectors.</p></p>
