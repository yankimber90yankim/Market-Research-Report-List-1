<p><p>The GPS simulator market is typically categorized into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel simulators are designed to emulate a single GPS satellite signal, primarily used for basic testing and application development. In contrast, multi-channel simulators can replicate multiple satellite signals simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive testing environment. This is essential for advanced applications such as autonomous vehicles and complex navigation systems, allowing developers to assess performance under various scenarios and improve reliability in real-world conditions.</p></p>
