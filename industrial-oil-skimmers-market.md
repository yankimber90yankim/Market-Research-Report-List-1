<p><p>The industrial oil skimmers market consists of various types designed to efficiently remove oil from water surfaces. Belt oil skimmers utilize a continuous belt to attract and separate oil, ideal for large volumes. Disk oil skimmers employ rotating disks to collect oil, providing effective separation in smaller or confined environments. These technologies are essential for maintaining environmental compliance and reducing pollution.</p></p>
