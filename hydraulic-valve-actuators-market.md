<p><p>The hydraulic valve actuators market comprises two main types: linear actuators and rotary actuators. Linear actuators convert hydraulic pressure into straight-line motion, commonly used for applications requiring precise control of valve position, such as in gate or globe valves. Rotary actuators, on the other hand, transform hydraulic energy into rotational motion, ideal for operating ball or butterfly valves. Both types are crucial in various industries, facilitating efficient fluid control and automation in processes like power generation, oil and gas, and manufacturing.</p></p>
