<p><p>The orbital welding equipment market encompasses various specialized types. Pipe orbital welding is primarily used for joining pipes in industries like oil and gas, ensuring strong, leak-proof connections. Fusion orbital welding focuses on creating welds without filler material, ideal for applications requiring minimal distortion and high purity, often seen in reactor and pharmaceutical sectors. Tube-to-tubesheet welding is essential in heat exchangers, facilitating efficient fluid transfer and thermal conduction by securely joining tubes to the sheet.</p></p>
