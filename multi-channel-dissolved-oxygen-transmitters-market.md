<p><p>Multi-channel dissolved oxygen transmitters are classified based on their wiring configurations: 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire systems. </p><p>- **2-Wire** systems are cost-effective and simple, using power and signal on the same pair of wires, ideal for basic applications. </p><p>- **3-Wire** systems provide improved signal integrity and are often used in more demanding environments. </p><p>- **4-Wire** configurations offer the highest accuracy and minimal interference, suitable for comprehensive monitoring in industrial settings, allowing for separate power and signal lines.</p></p>
