<p><strong>The IoT RFID Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID software market is primarily divided into two types: passive and active RFID. Passive RFID systems utilize tags that do not have their own power source, relying on energy from RFID readers to transmit data. These are cost-effective and ideal for inventory tracking. Active RFID, on the other hand, includes tags with their own power supply, allowing for longer read ranges and real-time data transmission, making them suitable for asset tracking in dynamic environments.</p></p>
