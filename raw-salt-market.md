<p><p>The raw salt market comprises various types, including sea salt, lake salt, well salt, and rock salt. Sea salt is harvested from evaporated seawater, prized for its minerals. Lake salt comes from saltwater lakes, featuring unique mineral profiles. Well salt is sourced through brine extraction from underground aquifers, while rock salt is mined from ancient salt deposits, often used for industrial purposes. Each type caters to different culinary, industrial, and health applications, shaping diverse segments within the overall raw salt market.</p></p>
