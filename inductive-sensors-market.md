<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inductive Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Inductive Type</li><li>Mutual Inductive Type</li><li>Eddy Current Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive sensors are key components in automation and industrial applications. The self-inductive type operates by detecting changes in its own magnetic field, suitable for non-metallic targets. Mutual inductive sensors work by measuring the interaction between their magnetic field and that of a target object, effective for metallic detection. Eddy current sensors utilize induced currents in conductive materials to assess proximity and material properties, offering high precision and reliability. Each type serves specific application needs in process control and object detection.</p></p>
