<p><ul><li>Differential Scanning Calorimeter</li><li>Oxygen Bomb Calorimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calorimeter market encompasses various types, including Differential Scanning Calorimeters (DSC) and Oxygen Bomb Calorimeters. DSC measures thermal transitions in materials, such as melting and crystallization, providing insights into material properties and behavior. In contrast, Oxygen Bomb Calorimeters assess the heat of combustion of substances, crucial for determining fuel energy content. Both instruments are vital in research and quality control across industries like pharmaceuticals, materials science, and energy, enabling precise thermal analysis and energy evaluation.</p></p>
