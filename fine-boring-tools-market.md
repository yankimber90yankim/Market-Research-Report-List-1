<p><p>The Fine Boring Tools market comprises two main types: Dedicated Diameters and Flexible Diameters. Conversely, Flexible Diameters offer adjustable sizes, enabling manufacturers to accommodate varied bore dimensions without the need for multiple tools. This versatility makes them suitable for tasks requiring customization and adaptability, catering to a wider range of applications in the machining industry.</p></p>
