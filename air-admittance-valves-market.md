<p><ul><li>Zero Pressure</li><li>Positive Pressure</li><li>Negative Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Admittance Valves (AAV) market is categorized into three types based on pressure conditions: Zero Pressure, Positive Pressure, and Negative Pressure. Zero Pressure AAVs operate without any pressure differential, allowing air intake when wastewater flows, preventing siphoning. Positive Pressure AAVs are designed to open under conditions of excess pressure, ensuring efficient venting. Negative Pressure AAVs function when there is a drop in pressure, facilitating airflow to maintain system balance. Each type serves distinct plumbing and ventilation applications.</p></p>
