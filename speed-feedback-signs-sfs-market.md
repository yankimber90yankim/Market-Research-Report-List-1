<p><p>Speed Feedback Signs (SFS) are traffic management devices designed to display real-time vehicle speeds to drivers, encouraging safer driving behavior by promoting adherence to speed limits. These signs are commonly used in school zones, residential areas, construction sites, and high-traffic zones to enhance road safety and reduce accidents. SFS typically use radar or laser technology to detect vehicle speeds and display them on LED screens, often accompanied by warnings or alerts if the speed exceeds the limit.</p><p>The Speed Feedback Signs (SFS) Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing urbanization, rising concerns about road safety, and stricter government regulations.
