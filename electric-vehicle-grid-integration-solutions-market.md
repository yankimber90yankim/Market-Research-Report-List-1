<p><p>Electric Vehicle Grid Integration Solutions encompass various market types, primarily focusing on Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) and Virtual Power Plant (VPP) models. V2G allows electric vehicles to return stored energy to the grid, enhancing energy management and providing backup power. Conversely, VPP aggregates multiple distributed energy resources, including EVs, to optimize energy production and consumption on a broader scale. Both concepts facilitate enhanced grid stability, improve renewable energy utilization, and enable distributed generation, benefiting overall energy efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
