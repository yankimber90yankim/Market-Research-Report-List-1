<p><p>RV reducers, or robotic reducers, are essential for automated systems, and they primarily come in two types: hollow type and flange output type. The hollow type features a central bore allowing for direct mounting on shafts, which aids in compact designs. In contrast, the flange output type offers a flat surface for connecting external components, enhancing stability and alignment. Both types optimize torque and speed, providing critical performance for various robotic applications, including industrial automation and precision movements.</p></p>
