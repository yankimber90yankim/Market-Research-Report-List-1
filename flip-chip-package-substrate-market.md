<p><p>The Flip-Chip Package Substrate market encompasses various packaging types, primarily FCBGA (Flip-Chip Ball Grid Array) and FCCSP (Flip-Chip Chip Scale Package). FCBGA features a grid of solder balls that facilitate efficient heat dissipation and electrical connectivity, making it suitable for high-performance applications. Conversely, FCCSP offers a compact design with minimal footprint, ideal for portable electronics. Both types leverage flip-chip technology for enhanced performance, enabling better electrical and thermal conductivity while meeting increasingly demanding device requirements in consumer electronics and automotive sectors.</p></p>
