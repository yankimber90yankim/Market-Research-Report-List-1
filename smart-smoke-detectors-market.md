<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Detector</li><li>Ionization Smoke Detector</li><li>Combination Smoke Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart smoke detectors market comprises three main types: Photoelectric smoke detectors, which use a light beam to detect smoke signals, offering quicker responses to smoldering fires. Ionization smoke detectors, on the other hand, contain radioactive material to sense fast-flaming fires, making them effective for quick ignition detection. Combination smoke detectors integrate both technologies, providing comprehensive protection by detecting various types of fires. Together, these options enhance safety through advanced sensing capabilities and connectivity features.</p></p>
