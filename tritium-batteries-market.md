<p><strong>The Tritium Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Batteries</li><li>Non-Thermal Conversion Batteries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tritium batteries market is divided into two main types: thermal conversion batteries and non-thermal conversion batteries. Thermal conversion batteries utilize heat generated from the decay of tritium to produce electricity, making them suitable for applications requiring sustained power over long periods. In contrast, non-thermal conversion batteries rely on direct electrochemical processes to generate energy without the need for heat. Each type serves distinct applications, contributing to advancements in energy storage solutions, particularly in remote and specialized environments.</p></p>
