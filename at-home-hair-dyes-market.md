<p><p>The at-home hair dye market is categorized into three main types: semi-permanent, permanent, and temporary hair dyes. Semi-permanent hair dyes offer a lasting color effect that gradually fades with washes, typically lasting several weeks. Permanent hair dyes provide long-lasting results by chemically altering the hair structure, ensuring vibrant color that endures until new hair growth occurs. Temporary hair dyes offer a short-term solution, lasting only a few washes, making them ideal for experimentation without commitment. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
