<p><p>The safety belt alarm harness market consists of three key types: slide version, mechanical version, and hall version. The slide version utilizes a sliding mechanism to detect seatbelt engagement, triggering an alarm when unbuckled. The mechanical version employs physical components to achieve similar functionality, often relying on lever systems. The hall version incorporates magnetic sensors that detect seatbelt status, providing a reliable and efficient alarm system. Each type aims to enhance safety in vehicles by alerting users about seatbelt usage.</p></p>
