<p><p>The hydraulic gear wheel pump market consists of two primary types: external gear pumps and internal gear pumps. External gear pumps utilize two interlocking gears to move hydraulic fluid, offering high efficiency and consistent flow. They are commonly used in various industrial applications for their simplicity and reliability. Internal gear pumps, on the other hand, feature a rotating gear inside a larger stationary gear, providing quieter operation and smoother flow. Both types cater to different needs based on pressure requirements and installation space.</p></p>
