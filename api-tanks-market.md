<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The API Tanks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Roof</li><li>External Floating Roof</li><li>Internal Floating Roof</li><li>Dome External Floating Roof</li></ul></p>
<p><p>API tanks are categorized based on their design and functionality in petroleum storage. Fixed roof tanks have a rigid structure, minimizing evaporation losses. External floating roof tanks feature a roof that floats on the liquid surface, reducing vapor emissions. Internal floating roof tanks contain a roof that sits inside the tank, providing additional protection against evaporation. Dome external floating roof tanks combine the benefits of floating roofs with a dome structure, enhancing storage integrity while further reducing emissions.</p></p>
