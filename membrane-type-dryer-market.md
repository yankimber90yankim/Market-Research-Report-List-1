<p><p>The Membrane Type Dryer market is segmented into two main types: porous and non-porous membranes. Porous membranes allow the selective passage of gases while retaining liquids, making them ideal for applications involving moisture removal from air or gas streams. Non-porous membranes, on the other hand, create a barrier that restricts gas flow, predominantly used for applications requiring high purity and separation efficiency. Both types cater to diverse industries, such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and petrochemical, enhancing efficiency and energy savings.</p></p>
