<p><p>The Medical High Temperature Sterilization Equipment Market comprises several types, primarily steam sterilization and dry sterilization. Steam sterilization utilizes pressurized steam to eliminate microorganisms, making it effective for heat and moisture-stable instruments. Dry sterilization, on the other hand, employs high temperatures without moisture, suitable for heat-sensitive tools and materials. Other methods may include chemical or gas sterilization techniques that cater to specific needs. Each type plays a critical role in ensuring safety and compliance in medical environments.</p></p>
