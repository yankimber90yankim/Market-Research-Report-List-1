<p><ul><li>Positive Developer</li><li>Negative Developer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The film developers market is divided into two main types: positive developers and negative developers. Positive developers are used to create direct positives from film, producing images that can be directly viewed, such as in slide film. In contrast, negative developers process film to create negatives, which require further printing to produce visible images. Positive developers cater to markets focused on unique presentations and archiving, while negative developers primarily serve traditional photography and commercial printing needs, each fulfilling distinct artistic and practical purposes.</p></p>
