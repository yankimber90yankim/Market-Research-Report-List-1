<p><p>Rooftop unit control markets are categorized into 2-loop and 4-loop units based on their control systems. A 2-loop unit typically manages temperature and pressure, providing basic climate control for smaller applications. In contrast, a 4-loop unit incorporates additional loops to control humidity and airflow, enabling more precise and efficient climate management. This differentiation allows for tailored solutions across various environments, ensuring optimal energy efficiency and comfort levels in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
