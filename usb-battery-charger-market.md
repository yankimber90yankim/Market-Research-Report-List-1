<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The USB Battery Charger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Downstream Port (SDP)</li><li>Charging Downstream Port (CDP)</li><li>Dedicated Charging Port (DCP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The USB battery charger market includes three primary types: Standard Downstream Port (SDP), Charging Downstream Port (CDP), and Dedicated Charging Port (DCP). SDP is used for data transfer and basic charging, typically delivering 500mA. CDP can provide 1.5A for faster charging while supporting data communication. DCP, on the other hand, is optimized solely for charging, offering higher current output (up to 2.1A or more), making it ideal for quick charging of devices without data transfer capabilities. Each type serves distinct user needs.</p></p>
