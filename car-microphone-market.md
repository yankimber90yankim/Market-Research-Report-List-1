<p><strong>The Car Microphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ECM Microphone</li><li>MEMS Microphone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car microphone market features two main types: ECM (Electret Condenser Microphone) and MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) microphones. ECM microphones are known for their high sensitivity and sound quality, making them ideal for voice recognition and hands-free communication in vehicles. In contrast, MEMS microphones are compact, durable, and capable of filtering background noise, which enhances clarity in audio capture. Both types are crucial for improving in-car communication systems, infotainment, and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS).</p></p>
